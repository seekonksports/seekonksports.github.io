What are we?

We are a team of passionate students from Seekonk Middle School working on a genius hour project. Our project focuses on creating a centralized website specifically for the High School's sports. At the moment, only Football is featured on our site, though we hope to include Ice Hockey soon! Currently, the scores and schedules for these sports are scattered across multiple websites, causing inconvenience and difficulty in accessing the information.

We aim to address this problem by developing a platform that gathers all the scores and schedules in one single place. By doing so, we hope to make it easier for students, parents, and fans to stay updated and engaged with Seekonk High School's football games.

What is our goal?

Our main goal is to simplify the process of finding information about Seekonk High School's football games. With our dedicated website, we want to provide a centralized platform where everyone interested in these sports can easily access the latest scores and schedules.

By consolidating the information and making it readily available, we aim to enhance the overall experience for students, parents, and fans. Our website will streamline the search for this information, eliminating the need to visit multiple sources and saving time and effort.

How will our website work?

Our website will have a user-friendly interface designed to provide a seamless experience for visitors. Here are some key features:

  • Scoreboard: We will display the scores for the football games very shortly after the event, allowing users to stay updated on recent matches.
  • Schedules: Users can easily access the upcoming game schedules for the team, helping you plan for your attendance.
  • Team Information: We will provide comprehensive information about Seekonk High School's football team, including player profiles, team statistics, and coaching staff details.
  • News and Updates: Visitors can find the latest news, highlights, and articles related to Seekonk High School's sports.
  • Community Engagement: We will incorporate interactive features such as forums or comment sections where users can discuss games, share their thoughts, and connect with fellow enthusiasts. Though this feature is not yet available, be ready for it soon!

Through these features, our website will serve as the one-stop hub for all things related to Seekonk High School's sports, fostering a sense of community and promoting engagement.

Get Involved!

We believe in the power of collaboration and welcome contributions from the Seekonk High School community. Here are some ways you can get involved:

  • Join Our Team: If you are interested in web development, content creation, or sports journalism, we would love to have you as part of our team. Together, we can continue improving and expanding our website.
  • Provide Feedback: As we strive to create a platform that meets the needs of our users, your feedback is invaluable. Let us know what features you find useful or suggest improvements to enhance the user experience.
  • Spread the Word: Help us reach a wider audience by sharing our website with your friends, family, and fellow students. The more people who benefit from our platform, the stronger our community becomes.

By actively participating and supporting our project, you can contribute to the success of our centralized website and help make Seekonk High School's hockey and football information easily accessible for everyone.

Our website is open source!

We are proud to say that our project is open source, which means that the entire source code for our project is freely available for anyone to view, modify, and contribute! Here's what this means:

  • Transparency
  • The opportunity for you to learn what goes into creating a website
  • This site is community-driven
  • Transparency

Interested in helping out?

  1. Visit our github repository by pressing the </> button on the nav menu
  2. Fork the repository to create your own copy
  3. Make your desired changes
  4. Submit a "pull request" to propose your changes towards the project

Shortly after, we will review your pull request and integrate it into the project! We highly value and appreciate all contributions, no matter how big or small. Together, we will make a meaningful impact on the Seekonk Highschool community!
