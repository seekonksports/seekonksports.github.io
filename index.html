What are we?

We are a team of passionate students from Seekonk High School working on a genius hour project. Our project focuses on creating a centralized website specifically for Seekonk High School's hockey and football sports. Currently, the scores and schedules for these sports are scattered across multiple websites, causing inconvenience and difficulty in accessing the information.

Our aim is to address this problem by developing a comprehensive platform that gathers all the scores and schedules in one place. By doing so, we hope to make it easier for students, parents, and fans to stay updated and engaged with Seekonk High School's hockey and football games.

What is our goal?

Our main goal is to simplify the process of finding information about Seekonk High School's hockey and football games. With our dedicated website, we want to provide a centralized platform where everyone interested in these sports can easily access the latest scores and schedules.

By consolidating the information and making it readily available, we aim to enhance the overall experience for students, parents, and fans. Our website will streamline the search for this information, eliminating the need to visit multiple sources and saving time and effort.

For more information, visit the About page linked above!

How will our website work?

Our website will have a user-friendly interface designed to provide a seamless experience for visitors. Here are some key features:

  • Scoreboard: We will display real-time scores for hockey and football games, allowing users to stay updated on ongoing matches.
  • Schedules: Users can easily access the upcoming game schedules for both hockey and football teams, helping them plan their attendance or viewing.
  • Team Information: We will provide comprehensive information about Seekonk High School's hockey and football teams, including player profiles, team statistics, and coaching staff details.
  • News and Updates: Visitors can find the latest news, highlights, and articles related to Seekonk High School's hockey and football sports.
  • Community Engagement: We will incorporate interactive features such as forums or comment sections where users can discuss games, share their thoughts, and connect with fellow enthusiasts.

Through these features, our website will serve as a one-stop hub for all things related to Seekonk High School's hockey and football, fostering a sense of community and promoting engagement.
